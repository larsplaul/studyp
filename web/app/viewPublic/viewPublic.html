What are Study Points?

On the Computer Science AP education (datamatiker) we use a system of study points, earned by participating in areas necessary to become a great student.

Students earn study points for:

  • Attending and participating in class
  • Individual exercises, necessary to improve personal skills
  • Group based projects, necessary to improve group and personal skills
  • Helping other Students

Generally Study Points is rewarded for a determined effort, in most cases not for the actual result

What Study Points Are Not

Study Points are NOT grades.
